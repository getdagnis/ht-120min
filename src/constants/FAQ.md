# Yellow warnings

## SHORT

The system only compares tournament fixtures against actual Hattrick friendlies. It does not try to determine intent or fault.

Because of that, teams may occasionally receive an automatic yellow warning even when there is a perfectly reasonable explanation.

For example, a teams is playing a friendly that was arranged before joining the tournament (possibly even before the tournament was created). The system would correctly detect that as "Misarranged", but realising the tournament didn't even exist at the time of scheduling that friendly, the warning would obviously be removed by an admin review.

The purpose of the system is not to punish managers automatically. Its purpose is to quickly identify fixtures that do not match the tournament schedule and bring them to everyone's attention. **Automatic system first. Human judgement second.**

### Why did I receive a yellow warning for a misarranged friendly?

The tournament automatically checks whether teams have scheduled the friendly specified by the tournament fixture list.

Example:

Tournament fixture:
Team A vs Team B

But the system finds:
Team A vs Team C

Result:
Team A receives a yellow warning and the fixture is marked as **Misarranged**.

### What if the friendly was scheduled before joining the tournament?

This can happen, especially during the first round of a new tournament.

If a team had already arranged a friendly before joining the tournament (or before the tournament even existed), the automated system may still flag it as misarranged.

In such cases the warning can be removed by an administrator.

### Why is the system designed this way?

The goal is to keep enforcement simple, transparent and automatic.

The system only compares:

- the tournament fixture list
- the actual friendly scheduled in Hattrick

It does not try to guess intentions or determine fault.

This allows managers to immediately see when a fixture has not been scheduled correctly and react before future rounds are affected.

### Will every yellow warning result in punishment?

No.

Yellow warnings are primarily a visibility and tracking tool.

Tournament administrators may remove warnings when there is a clear and reasonable explanation, such as:

- Friendly arranged before joining the tournament
- Friendly arranged before the tournament was created
- Technical issues or exceptional circumstances
- Other situations accepted by tournament administrators

### What should I do if I think a warning is unfair?

Raise the issue in the tournament chat or contact the tournament administrators.

The system makes automatic decisions.

Admins make final decisions.
